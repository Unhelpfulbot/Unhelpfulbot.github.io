Marshmellow Launcher

Using Solidworks, created a general 3D model of the launcher's design then gather parts from the workshops and hardware stores. The team decided to use a pvc motor design, so the accuracy of the launcher would be more consistent. As a result, the design requires a strong foundation to counteract the recoil from launching. With the final design, parts were machined into CAD dimensions and then assembled. Modifcation on the launcher were required after testing the launcher, espically when it came to the launch angle.
